Our Story

Born on a rainy evening,
under one umbrella.

Monsoon, 2016. Two friends, one shared umbrella, and a street-side burger that somehow tasted better in the rain. That night we made a promise — to build a place where every burger feels like shelter: warm, generous, and impossible to forget.

Today, Umbrella Burger is Bandra's slow-obsessed burger house. We smash our patties by hand every morning, bake our brioche in-house every four hours, and finish every plate under low amber light — the way it deserves.
